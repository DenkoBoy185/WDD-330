# W03 Final Project: Brainstorm Ideas

Here are four web application project ideas, each utilizing a third-party API:

## 1. Travel Weather & Packing Planner
*   **Description:** A travel application where a user inputs their destination and travel dates. The app displays the current and forecasted weather for that location, suggests local points of interest, and generates a dynamic packing list based on the expected weather conditions (e.g., suggesting an umbrella if rain is forecasted).
*   **Third-Party API:** [OpenWeatherMap API](https://openweathermap.org/api) (for weather forecasting data).

## 2. Pantry to Plate: Recipe Finder & Nutritional Analyzer
*   **Description:** A culinary web app that helps reduce food waste. Users enter the ingredients they currently have in their kitchen, and the app returns a list of recipes they can make. When a recipe is selected, it displays step-by-step instructions and a detailed nutritional breakdown. Users can also save their favorite recipes.
*   **Third-Party API:** [Spoonacular Recipe and Food API](https://spoonacular.com/food-api) or [Edamam Recipe Search API](https://developer.edamam.com/edamam-recipe-api).

## 3. "Watch Next" Movie & TV Show Tracker
*   **Description:** An entertainment tracking application where users can search for movies and TV shows, add them to a "Watchlist", and rate them after viewing. The app provides detailed information including cast, crew, trailers, and current streaming platforms where the content is available. It can also offer personalized recommendations based on saved items.
*   **Third-Party API:** [The Movie Database (TMDB) API](https://developer.themoviedb.org/docs/getting-started).

## 4. Virtual Stock Portfolio Simulator
*   **Description:** A personal finance educational app that allows users to practice trading stocks without financial risk. Users are given a virtual starting balance and can "buy" and "sell" shares of real companies based on live market data. The dashboard displays their portfolio's performance over time, daily gains/losses, and recent financial news relevant to their holdings.
*   **Third-Party API:** [Finnhub Stock API](https://finnhub.io/) or [Alpha Vantage API](https://www.alphavantage.co/).
